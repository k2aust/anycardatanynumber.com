Data Deletion Request

Effective date: March 20, 2026  |  Last updated: March 20, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of any data associated with the Kevin The Magician video publishing application and its use of the TikTok API.

What Data Is Held

This application is a personal automation tool. The only data held in connection with TikTok is:

  • OAuth access and refresh tokens that authorize the app to post on behalf of the account.
  • Basic TikTok profile information (username and user ID) used solely to confirm the correct account.

No data from viewers, followers, or any other third parties is collected or stored.

How to Request Data Deletion

To revoke access and have all associated tokens deleted, you can:

Option 1 – Revoke directly on TikTok:
  1. Open TikTok and go to Profile → Settings → Security → Authorized apps.
  2. Locate Kevin The Magician and tap Revoke access.
  3. All OAuth tokens held by this app are immediately invalidated.
Option 2 – Email request:
  1. Send an email to k2aust@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your TikTok username so the correct tokens can be identified and deleted.
  3. Deletion will be confirmed within 30 days.

After Deletion

Once access is revoked or a deletion request is fulfilled:

  • All stored OAuth tokens for your account are permanently deleted.
  • The application will no longer be able to access or post to your TikTok account.
  • No backups or copies of the tokens are retained.
